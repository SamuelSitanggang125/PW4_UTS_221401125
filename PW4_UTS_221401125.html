My Favourite Anime

Fuutarou Uesugi

Fuutarou Uesugi is the main protagonist of the 5-toubun no Hanayome series. He is a student at Asahiyama High School who lives with his father, Isanari Uesugi, and younger sister, Raiha Uesugi, in a small apartment.

Nakano Ichika

Ichika Nakano is one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is one of the quintuplet sisters, with the other four being Nino, Miku, Yotsuba, and Itsuki Nakano. Ichika is the eldest of the siblings and serves as one of the main love interests for the protagonist, Fuutarou Uesugi.

Nakano Nino

Nino Nakano is one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is one of the quintuplet sisters, along with Ichika, Miku, Yotsuba, and Itsuki Nakano. Nino is the second eldest of the siblings and serves as one of the main love interests for the protagonist, Fuutarou Uesugi.

Nakano Miku

Miku Nakano is the one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is one of the quintuplet sisters, along with Ichika, Nino, Yotsuba, and Itsuki Nakano. Miku is the third eldest of the siblings and serves as one of the main love interests for the protagonist, Fuutarou Uesugi.

Nakano Yotsuba

Yotsuba Nakano is one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is the fourth sibling among her quintuplet sisters, with the other four being Ichika, Nino, Miku, and Itsuki Nakano. Yotsuba serves as one of the main love interests for the protagonist, Fuutarou Uesugi.

Nakano Itsuki

Itsuki Nakano is one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is the youngest of the quintuplet sisters, along with Ichika, Nino, Miku, and Yotsuba Nakano. She is the first quintuplet that the protagonist Fuutarou Uesugi encounters, and the two have a complicated relationship throughout the series.
